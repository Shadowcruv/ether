The Story

Thorn, Bukkie, Ember, Kate, and John were called upon to defend a village from a horde of demons and monsters. They fought fiercely, each utilizing their unique abilities to take down the creatures. Thorn launched her thorns at the demons, while Bukkie summoned a swarm of butterflies to distract them. Ember's fiery fists struck the monsters with incredible force, and Kate's accurate shooting took out several demons at once. John charged into the fray, taking down the biggest and toughest monsters with his incredible strength. Together, they were an unstoppable force, and they soon emerged victorious.
